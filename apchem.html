AP Chemistry Summer Assignment

The summer homework with links that you can click on, so cramming is ever so easy! - Henry

Welcome to AP Chemistry! The fact that you have decided to take AP Chemistry indicates that you have both the skills and the work ethic necessary for success. In an effort to keep what you have learned this year current, as well as prep you for the new material to be learned next year, you will need to complete the following assignment. In reality, the more you practice this, the better you will do. If that is not enough to motivate you, you can bank on a test covering this material the first week of school. It would probably benefit you to take good notes on the material in a journal reserved for this purpose. Please know that I appreciate your hard work in advance, and look forward to working with you next year.
